# Supervisor Planning

## Deliverables

- 2-3 candidate directions
- explicit rationale for each direction
- dependencies between directions
- what evidence would keep, merge, or kill each direction
- sequencing for literature, experiment, and review checkpoints

## Planning Rule

Do not assign work until the direction has:

1. a clear question
2. a visible baseline or comparison target
3. a realistic first artifact
