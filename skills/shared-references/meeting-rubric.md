# Meeting Rubric

## PREPARE

- clear state of progress
- key blockers
- proposed next move

## CROSS-READ

- all required artifacts read
- disagreements identified before writing critiques

## CHALLENGE

- critique targets evidence and reasoning, not tone
- every criticism proposes a stronger alternative or missing check

## RESPOND

- answers each material criticism
- distinguishes accepted fixes from defended choices

## DECISION

- per-agent direction is explicit
- next tasks are actionable
- trigger for next meeting is concrete
